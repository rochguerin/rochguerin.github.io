Motivations and Scope

  • Keeping the cost of QoS routing contained requires operation with coarse triggering policies

    • Inaccuracy in the information used by QoS routing
    • Impact on routing performance

  • Can we design algorithms that are robust to information inaccuracy?

  • Premise of this work

    • Explicitly incorporate knowledge that information is inaccurate in path selection algorithm
      • Safety aware routing
      • Randomized path selection
