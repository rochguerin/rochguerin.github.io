Focus of This Talk

  • Investigate the feasibility of QoS routing

    • What are the major cost factors?
    • What are the potential performance gains?
    • Trade-off between the two?

  • Three prong approach

    • Simulation based investigation of link state protocol
      • Explore basic design issues and trade-off
    • Implementation of OSPF extensions on GateD
      • Benchmarking of actual costs (processor, memory, etc.)
    • Design of algorithm aimed at enhancing cost-performance trade-off
      • Safety aware routing
      • Randomized path selection
