Conclusions

  • QoS routing is more expensive, but

    • Added cost is containable
    • Many knobs are available to tune it (if needed!)
    • It does offer performance (and functionality) benefits

  • Other factor (forwarding, signalling) are more likely to be the show-stoppers

  • Extensions

    • Multi-area solution (aggregation)
    • Combined solution for traffic engineering and on-demand routing
