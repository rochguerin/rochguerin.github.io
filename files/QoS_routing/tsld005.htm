Other “QoS” Routing Models

  • “QoS” routing for traffic engineering

    • Goal is to select paths and assign traffic to “optimize” global network performance measure

  • Similarities and Differences with “traditional” QoS routing model

    • No explicit requests but relies on measured traffic
    • Different optimization criteria
    • Lesser impact on core router (resource monitoring)
    • Similar impact on forwarding and signalling
    • Likely convergence/coexistence, e.g., routing of long-lived flows (Sigcomm’99 paper by Shaikh et al.)
