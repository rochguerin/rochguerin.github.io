Motivation and Goals

  • Networks and applications are becoming more capable

    • Int-Serv/Diff-Serv, multimedia, video streaming, IP telephony, etc.

  • But routing protocols have barely evolved

  • Renewed interest in exploring various enhancements to routing protocols

    • Constraint-based routing, QoS routing, etc.

  • Natural question is whether the added functionality is worth it

    • Cost-performance trade-off?
