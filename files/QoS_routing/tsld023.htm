Observations

  • No major surprises

    • Strong correlation between update cost and performance
    • But performance advantages can persist in some low cost regions

  • Need to adapt triggering policy to operating environment

    • The larger the hold-down timer, the coarser the triggering policy
      • Avoid being “stuck” with stale information
    • Use of quantized values with coarse triggering policies

  • Network topology has a major influence on routing performance

    • Number of alternate paths
    • Sensitivity to trunk reservation parameters
